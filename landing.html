â€¯Overview of Process and Creating a Study:
  1. Confirm (or-reconfirm) the attestation and complete the form in the Study Creator
  2. Your study draft will be reviewed by vi-usertestingreviewers@microsoft.com
  3. Once approved, your test will be created and executed at your requested start time
  4. Your study drafts will be saved here
  5. For moderated studies:
    1. The first executed test will be for 2-partipants to pilot your study and review if any changes are needed before expanding to a larger audience
    2. Once your pilot study is completed, we will expand it to the remaining participants you requested (there is a maximum of 15 participants per study)
  6. For Moderated studies
    1. We will schedule an interview with 1 participant to pilot your study and review if any changes are needed before expanding to a larger audience
    2. Once your pilot study is completed, we will expand it to the remaining participants you requested (there is a maximum of 5 participants per study)
  7. You may save your study analysis here and ensure there is no PII (personal identifiable information)
